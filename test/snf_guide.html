STOCK ENTRY GUIDELINES

Please note that the sachets you will enter are not from packed boxes. Only enter the sachets that are bring out from boxes after unpacking for SAM/MAM.

Available Stock

Very first time stock to be entered in available stock. No sachets only boxes available in FC will be entered.

Recieved Stock

From second time and onward the boxes received from ware-house should be entered.

Opening Stock(Boxes)

Disabled and Auto calculated field to just show the opening stock for current day.

Opening Stock(Sachets)

Very first time it will be enabled to enter available sachets, after ward it will be auto calculated field to just show the opening stock of sachets for current day.

Distributed Stock(Boxes)

Always enabled field to enter the distributed boxes on that day.

Distributed Stock(Sachets)

Only enter the sachets that were bring out from boxes for SAM/MAM case on that day.

Closing Stock(Boxes)

Disabled field to show information of stock Boxes left in FC after receiving and distributing.

Closing Stock(Sachets)

Disabled field to show information of Sachets out from boxes remaining in FC after receiving and distributing.

Remarks

Your comments about issues in stock or no distribution on the day. Or any other abnormalities detected in stock.
